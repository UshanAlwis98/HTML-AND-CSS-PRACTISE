If you cannot access your previous profile due to a mobile number change or vehicle ownership transfer, you may use the 'Vehicle Registration' option to transfer the profile to your name. Please visit the 'Vehicle Registration' section and complete the required steps. Once your registration is submitted, you will receive an option to delete the previous profile. Kindly review and validate the details before submitting your request.

If you plan to change your mobile number and have registered using your old number, log in with the old number (OTP) and select “Delete Profile” first. Then re-register using your new mobile number to create a new QR code.

Things to note prior to Registration

  1. Ensure all information is accurate before submission.
  2. Keep your vehicle's Certificate of Registration handy.
